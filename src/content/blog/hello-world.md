Welcome to **kafkade.com** — my corner of the internet.

## What is this?

This is where I'll write about software, systems, tools, and the ideas that connect them. Think of it as a journal — part technical, part reflective.

## Why "kafkade"?

The name comes from two things I care about:

- **Kafka** — Franz Kafka, the writer. His work explores the absurd structures we build and the ways we navigate them. Software isn't so different.
- **Cascade** — The mountain range near where I live. Mountains are patient, enduring, and quietly magnificent. Good software should aspire to the same.

## What to expect

I'll write when I have something worth sharing. No schedule, no filler. Topics will likely include:

- Tools I'm building or using
- Lessons from real projects
- Thoughts on the craft of software

If any of that sounds interesting, stick around. You can also follow along via [RSS](/rss.xml).
