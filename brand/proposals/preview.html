kafkade — Logo Proposals

Minimal single triangle. Pure geometry, amber summit accent.

Descending blocks like a waterfall. The "cascade" in kafkade, literally.

#4 — Stacked Chevrons

#5 — Peak + Horizon

Single peak above a horizon line. Clean, anchored, confident.

#6 — Twin Peaks

Two peaks sharing a valley. Dual amber accents — Kafka + Cascade.

#7 — Cascade Flow

Flowing curved lines suggesting falling water. More abstract/organic.

#8 — Outlined Peak

Thick-stroke outline only, no fill. Modern/minimal with inner reflection.

Open book where spines form mountain curves. Kafka (literature) + Cascade.

#10 — Diamond Peak

Rotated square forming a geometric peak. Bold, modern, distinctive.
