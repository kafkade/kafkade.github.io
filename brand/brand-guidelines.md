# kafkade — Brand Guidelines

## Origin

**kafkade** = Kafka (Franz Kafka, the author) + Cascade (the mountain range).
A software creator building at the intersection of thoughtful engineering and the Pacific Northwest.

---

## Identity

- **Name**: kafkade
- **Tagline**: "software, systems, and other things"
- **Voice**: Technical but approachable. Understated. Honest. A builder who reads.

---

## Color System (RockON!)

| Role       | Name      | Hex       | Usage                                   |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|------------------------------------------|
| Deep      | Midnight  | `#010221` | Dark mode background                     |
| Primary   | Ember     | `#C43302` | **Primary accent** — links, active states |
| Secondary | Teal      | `#0A7373` | Tags, code highlights, secondary accent  |
| Neutral   | Sage      | `#B7BF99` | Muted text, dates, meta                  |
| Highlight | Amber     | `#EDAA25` | Special emphasis, logo accent dot         |

### Light Mode (default)
- Background: `#F8F7F4` (warm paper white)
- Text: `#1A1A2E`
- Muted text: `#6B6B7B`
- Borders: `#E0DED8`

### Dark Mode
- Background: `#010221` (midnight)
- Text: `#E8E8E0`
- Muted text: `#B0AEA0`
- Borders: `#1A1F23`

---

## Typography

**Single font**: JetBrains Mono Variable — used for everything (headings, body, nav, code).
Full monospace, hacker aesthetic.

---

## Design Philosophy — "Terminal Journal"

Inspired by [マリウス (xn--gckvb8fzb.com)](https://xn--gckvb8fzb.com/):
- Light theme default
- No cards, no gradients, no blur, no shadows
- Content as plain text with good spacing
- Underlined links with inverted hover (bg fills, colors swap)
- Social links as text separated by `·`
- Max content width: 720px
- Generous line-height (1.65)

---

## Avatar

**Terminal design** — a terminal window on midnight background with colored code lines (ember, teal, amber, sage) and a blinking cursor. Used as profile picture across all platforms.

File: `brand/social/avatar-512.svg` (512×512, works for all platforms)

---

## Logo Concept

**Stacked chevrons** — nested V shapes receding into distance with an amber dot summit. Used in social banners and as a subtle watermark. Not displayed on the website itself (the name "kafkade" in monospace is the primary mark).
